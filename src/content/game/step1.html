First step: Understand Dumpfun.io

Dumpfun is a memecoin launchpad where the rules of the game have been completely rewritten.


Every token starts its life behind a lock—a ticking clock that forces everyone to hold.

Buy all you want, build your stack, but remember: the sell button doesn't exist yet.

When time runs out, the gates open. The lock breaks, and the "dump" begins as everyone races for the exit at the exact same moment.


It sounds like pure chaos and it is.
But that’s exactly why it works.
